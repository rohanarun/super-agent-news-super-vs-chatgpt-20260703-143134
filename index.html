Why computer‑using agents matter right now

The industry is still early

Even Big Tech leaders acknowledge that agentic AI is progressing more slowly than expected, especially when it comes to reliable execution of real tasks. That gap between demos and dependable operation is where product design matters most.

Source: channelnewsasia.com, techcrunch.com

Automation is arriving — cautiously

ChatGPT’s Scheduled Tasks show how mainstream assistants are easing into automation. Reviews highlight that these features are useful, but still limited compared to full computer‑driven workflows.

Source: en.softonic.com, androidauthority.com

Computer use is becoming first‑class

Google has made direct computer use a core capability in Gemini 3.5 Flash, underscoring how valuable real browser and desktop control is becoming for AI agents.

Source: blog.google, memeburn.com
